Homemade Lasagna

Description

This homemade lasagna recipe is rich, cheesy, and loaded with layers of flavorful sauce, tender pasta, and melted cheese. It's the perfect comfort food for family dinners or special occasions.

Made with a classic combination of ground beef, tomato sauce, and ricotta cheese, this dish is sure to impress. Serve it with garlic bread and a fresh salad for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz tomato sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add tomato sauce, oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg and Parmesan cheese.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, then a layer of noodles, followed by a layer of ricotta mixture and shredded mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella and bake for 45 minutes, until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  8. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
