Honey Glazed Salmon

Description

This honey glazed salmon is sweet, savory, and cooked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Mix honey, soy sauce, olive oil, garlic, and pepper.
  2. Marinate salmon for 15 minutes.
  3. Cook salmon in a pan over medium heat until golden and cooked through.
