Chicken

Description

A simple and flavorful chicken dish made with garlic, butter, and herbs.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and oregano.
  2. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Cook chicken for 5-7 minutes per side until golden and cooked through.
  4. Add garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
  5. Drizzle with lemon juice and serve.
